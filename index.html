About This Site

GPU Power Hub helps gamers and beginners understand how powerful different graphics cards are. You can explore power usage, performance levels, and see visuals of popular GPUs.

Understanding GPU Power

Wattage (TDP)

GPU power is measured in watts. Higher wattage usually means more performance, but also more heat.

Performance

More power allows higher FPS, better graphics, and smoother gameplay in modern games.

Cooling

Powerful GPUs need good cooling. Always pair high-end GPUs with proper airflow.
